Banaue Rice Terraces

Banaue rice terraces. Banaue rice terraces, system of irrigated rice terraces in the mountains of north-central Luzon, Philippines, that were created more than 2,000 years ago by the Ifugao people. Although located in several villages, they are collectively known as the Banaue rice terraces.

Taal Volcano

Taal is a municipality in Batangas. The old downtown (Poblacion) is noted for its Spanish colonial architecture, creating a feeling of nostalgia for the Spanish-era Philippines. The municipality's poblacion (downtown) is designated as a National Historical Landmark by the Philippine government.
